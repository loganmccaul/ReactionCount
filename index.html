Discover how your team reacts to your Slack messages! Reaction Count scans all of your Slack messages for reactions and then tallies up each reaction your messages have received.

Reaction Count needs access to search messages in Slack as your user. Reaction Count does not save any data, or track any requests. However, you may still be concerned about privacy, you can take a look at the source code.

Additionally, if you are still concerned about giving access to your Slack messages, you can create your own Slack app and deploy the source code on your own Vercel free tier account.
