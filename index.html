Reaction Count fetches all of your Slack messages with a reaction and then counts each reaction providing you with an overview of how your team reacts to your Slack messages.

Reaction Count needs access to search messages on your behalf, view reactions to a message, and view custom emojis in your Slack organization. Reaction Count does not save any data, or track any requests or responses. If you are concerned about privacy, you can review the source code on Github.

Additionally, if you are still concerned about privacy, you can fork the Slack app and deploy the source code on your own Vercel free tier account.
